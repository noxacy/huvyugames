Plant & Grow: Chill & Simulator

In Plant & Grow, you grow your farm and sell plants for profit. Developed by Noxacy & Huzeyfe, this game is an idle relaxing game that can be played on background.

Pro Tip: Calculate it's profit before you buy a plant instead of buying random plants.

Engine & Performance

Built with a high-precision Python/Pygame framework and exported via WebAssembly, Plant & Grow is an optimized farming experience.

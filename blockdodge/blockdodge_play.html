BLOCKDODGE V1.0

Blockdodge: Rhythm & Reflex

Sync your pulse with the beat in the ultimate test of survival. In Blockdodge, you control a lone entity in a world of aggressive geometry. Developed by Noxacy & Huzeyfe, this game challenges you to navigate through chaotic bullet-hell patterns that move in perfect harmony with the soundtrack.

Pro Tip: Don't just watch the shapes; feel the rhythm. The most complex "Block Storms" are designed to follow the musical BPM. Mastering the track is the key to mastering the stage.

Rhythm Mastery Guide

Surviving the geometric onslaught requires more than just fast hands; you need a strategic ear:

  • The Beat-Drop Dash: Patterns often intensify during the chorus. Save your focus for these high-energy segments where the squares move faster and in tighter formations.
  • Edge Control: While the center gives you the most movement options, the edges can be used to bait projectiles. Just be careful not to get cornered during a 360-degree sweep.
  • Music: This game uses a special remix of "Sonic Blaster - F-777" that was made by Noxacy.

Engine & Performance

Built with a high-precision Python/Pygame framework and exported via WebAssembly, Blockdodge ensures sub-millisecond input response, critical for high-tier rhythm gameplay.
